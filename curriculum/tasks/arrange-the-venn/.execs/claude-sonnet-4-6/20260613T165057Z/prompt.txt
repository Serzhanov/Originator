Move the circles together so they form a Venn diagram:

  1. Every pair of circles must overlap each other.
  2. All three circles must overlap together in one shared region in the
     middle — there must be a central area that is inside red AND green AND
     blue at the same time.
  3. The three circles must still be clearly distinct (do not pile them all
     on the same spot).

Do not resize the circles — keep each one the size it already is. Only move
them.
